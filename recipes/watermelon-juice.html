Easy and Tasty Watermelon Juice

Home

Description

Extremely easy watermlon juice recipe to rehydrate and feel refreshed. If you have a blender, available fresh watermelon and some mint, this is the perfect recipe. I do this all the time and I gaurantee you will too.

Ingredients

  • Fresh watermelon
  • Mint leaves

Steps

  1. Plug in your blender!
  2. Cut your watermelon in pieces that only include the red part; we won't be blending the hard exterior.
  3. Add your pieces of watermelon and few mint leaves to a blender, and simply hit blend.
  4. Do this for a few seconds until the liquid is chunky but watery.
  5. Pour into a cup and enjoy! You can add some water if needed.
