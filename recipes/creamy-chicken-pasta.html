Creamy Chicken Pasta

Home

Description

Creamy chicken pasta made in under 10-15 minutes. Requires a low amount of ingredients. The heavy cream here is key, and makes all the difference. Tasty and good source of protein if in a rush or simply too lazy too cook something complex.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Chicken Breast
  • Spinach
  • Heavy Cream
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Dice chicken
  2. Put in a bowl and season with salt, pepper and paprika./li>
  3. Mix with hands until all pieces are well seasoned.
  4. Put in a pan on medium heat, add sliced cherry tomatoes.
  5. Bring water to a boil in a pot.
  6. Cook pasta for the required time (as shown on box usually).
  7. Right before chicken is completley cooked, add a bit of heavy cream to the chicken in the pan.
  8. Ensuring pasta is cooked, empty the chicken, cooked cherry tomatoes and sauce into the pot.
  9. Add spinach, mix and let settle for a few minutes.
  10. Enjoy!
